How to Play Kalaha

Objective

Collect more seeds in your store than your opponent.

Gameplay

  • Pick a pit on your side and distribute seeds counterclockwise
  • Skip your opponent's store when sowing
  • Landing in your store grants an extra turn
  • Landing in an empty pit on your side captures opposite seeds

Winning

Game ends when one side is empty. Collect remaining seeds and count!
